Our Story

Our story: a passion for pizza, a journey of flavors and tradition that accompanies us every day.

Our story began in 1947, in the heart of Puglia, when our founder, driven by a love for Italian culinary tradition, opened a small neighborhood pizzeria.

With a wood-fired oven and recipes passed down from generation to generation, he created a place where the aroma of freshly baked pizza drew families and friends together to share special moments.

Today, after more than 70 years of passion, we continue to uphold that same dedication to quality and authenticity.

Every pizza we serve is a tribute to our history, made with the same genuine ingredients and love for tradition that has made our restaurant a landmark for those who love true Italian pizza.
